How To Make Freckle Juice


Why Would I Want To Make Freckle Juice?

Because some guy on the Internet gave you a recipe.



Ingredients

  • 1 cup grape juice
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper, or more to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder, or more to taste

Recipe instructions:

  1. Gather the ingredients (there are a lot, so it make take a while).
  2. Mix in one of those shaker-bottles you see everywhere.
  3. Chug!Chug!Chug!
